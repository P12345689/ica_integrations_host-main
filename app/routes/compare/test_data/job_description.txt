Job Title: Senior Full-Stack Developer

About Us:
TechInnovate is a rapidly growing tech company specializing in building scalable, cloud-native applications for enterprise clients. We're looking for a talented Senior Full-Stack Developer to join our dynamic team and help drive our technology initiatives forward.

Responsibilities:

- Strong knowledge of version control systems (Git)
- Excellent problem-solving and communication skills

Preferred Qualifications:

- Knowledge of machine learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- Familiarity with CI/CD practices and tools (Jenkins, GitLab CI)
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Relevant certifications (AWS, Google Cloud, Kubernetes)

What We Offer:

- Flexible work arrangements
- Collaborative and innovative work environment
- Chance to work on cutting-edge technologies and challenging projects
